Our Services

MyNon-Profit offers a range of digital bookkeeping services using MS Excel, QuickBooks and Google Cloud Computing to non-profit organizations in townships that are struggling with bookkeeping. Our services are tailored to meet the specific needs of Charities, Early Childhood Development (ECD) centres, religious organizations, and other similar non-profit entities. We understand that most organizations often have limited resources and may not have dedicated accounting staff. Therefore, we provide comprehensive support in setting up and managing their bookkeeping systems.

MyNon-Profit Digital Bookkeeping Setup

We help non-profit organizations set up digital bookkeeping systems using MS Excel, QuickBooks and Google Cloud Computing. This includes creating customized templates, establishing chart of accounts, and implementing efficient workflows.

MyNon-Profit Training & Support

We offer training sessions and ongoing support to non-profit organizations, empowering their staff to effectively manage their bookkeeping processes using MS Excel, QuickBooks and cloud computing tools. We ensure that organizations have the necessary skills to maintain accurate financial records.

MyNon-Profit Expense Tracking & Budgeting

We assist non-profit organizations in tracking their expenses, categorizing them appropriately, and comparing them against budgeted amounts. This helps in monitoring financial performance, identifying cost-saving opportunities, and ensuring funds are allocated efficiently.
